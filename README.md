## 🚀 Overview
MBF is a high-performance Hypixel Skyblock QOL bot focused entirely on **Bazaar flipping automation**. Unlike traditional Auction House macros or Forge/Fabric mods, MBF is built on **Mineflayer**, operating as a lightweight, headless client. 

This architecture allows for 24/7 uptime, incredibly low resource consumption, and remote management through a clean Web Dashboard—making your flipping workflow cleaner and significantly reducing risk.

## ✨ Key Features
* **Headless Architecture:** Built on Mineflayer, requiring no heavy Minecraft client to run.
* **Remote Control:** Fully manageable via an intuitive Web Dashboard.
* **Advanced Market Filters:** Configure custom profit thresholds, minimum margins, volume checks, and targeted item selection.
* **Smart Auto-Relisting:** Completely automated Bazaar order management and relisting workflows.
* **Event Integrations:** Real-time event logging, notifications, and snipe alerts pushed directly to your dashboard.
* **Low Overhead:** Runs comfortably on just ~1 GB of RAM per instance.

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

<details>
<summary><b>Is MBF an AH macro?</b></summary>
No. MBF is strictly focused on Bazaar flipping. Controlling it through a remote dashboard makes the workflow cleaner and less risky than traditional AH macros.
</details>

<details>
<summary><b>Is MBF a mod?</b></summary>
No. MBF is not a mod. It is built on Mineflayer, a headless library for Minecraft.
</details>

<details>
<summary><b>Does MBF require a separate Minecraft account?</b></summary>
No, but many users prefer using a dedicated alt account for automation.
</details>

<details>
<summary><b>Will MBF make profit automatically?</b></summary>
While MBF automates the mechanics of Bazaar flipping, your actual profit depends on market conditions, your configuration filters, competition, account limits, and starting capital.
</details>

<details>
<summary><b>Do I need prior Bazaar flipping experience?</b></summary>
No! MBF is extremely beginner-friendly and straightforward to install/configure, though basic Bazaar knowledge will help you optimize your custom filters for maximum profit.
</details>
